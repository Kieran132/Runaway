Taproom, Beer Garden & Bottle Shop

TAPROOM OPENING HOURS

Thurs 1 - 10 pm | Friday & Saturday 1 - 10:30 pm

Astley Street Stockport SK4 1AW

We love to share our passion for brewing and have built an informal Taproom and Beer Garden for you to enjoy our beer direct from the source.

Our taproom is designed to showcase our beer, with 10 lines of keg and 2 cask available each week, plus a wide range of bottled beer to drink in or take away. We also offer wine, independent cider, and soft drinks from Steep Soda.

We are currently operating card-only payments. Well-behaved children and dogs (on a lead) are welcome in the Taproom and Beer Garden.

Our taproom is less than 10 minutes' walk from Stockport Station (rail) and just a stone's throw from the bus interchange. Stockport is easily accessible from central Manchester with regular train services from Piccadilly taking around 10 minutes.
