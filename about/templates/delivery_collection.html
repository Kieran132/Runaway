Delivery & Collection

NATIONWIDE HOME DELIVERY (UK)

All orders are shipped on Mondays or Thursdays each week (except bank holidays when it'll be sent the next working day).

Our delivery service is via APC, who operate an overnight, fragile/liquid service. Parcels should reach the addressee by 4pm, but please be aware it may be slightly later, particularly during busy times of the year.

UK Mainland (excl. Scottish Highlands)

Orders received by midday Thursday are shipped for next working day delivery (Excludes Public Holidays). Orders placed after 12pm on Thursday are shipped on Monday, the following week.

Rest of UK

We offer shipping to the Isle of Wight, Scottish Highlands & Scottish Offshore on a 2-3 day service, dispatched on Thursday each week.

Unfortunately, we are currently unable to offer home delivery in Northern Ireland. We hope to resolve this situation soon.

AGE RESTRICTION

You must be 18 or older to buy alcohol in the UK. If you are buying alcohol for someone else, the recipient also has to be 18 or older. By placing an order with us online, you are confirming you are 18 or older. It is an offence to sell alcohol to anyone under the age of 18 in the UK.

Deliveries must be accepted by a person aged 18 or older. If our couriers are in any doubt about the age of the recipient on delivery, they will request a suitable form of ID. If an acceptable form of ID is not available to prove their age, delivery will be refused.

Collections from the brewery must be made by a person aged 18 or over; we operate a 'Challenge 25' policy and if we are in any doubt, we will request a suitable form of ID.

Suitable forms of ID are passport, photo card driving licence, an HM Forces warrant card, or a card bearing the PASS hologram.

We reserve the right to cancel the delivery if the age and identity of the recipient are in doubt.

CLICK AND COLLECT (LOCAL ORDERS)

Order your beer online and select to collect it from our Brewery Bottle Shop on Astley Street, Stockport (SK4 1AW) free of charge. Please allow 24 hours between placing your order and making collection, unless otherwise agreed with the brewery. Collections can be made during business hours (Mon to Fri 10am - 4pm).

If you have a question about your order, please contact us by email: sales@therunawaybrewery.com.
