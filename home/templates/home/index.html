Runaway Brewery makes great tasting modern British beer

We are independently owned and brew by hand in small batches at our microbrewery in central Stockport. Flavour and quality come first.

As brewers, we respect brewing tradition though our beer is contemporary in style.

As drinkers, we want to enjoy every beer that we make. For us, brewing is not just about the end product, it's about the process, so it is worth doing right.

Our Beer

We brew a range of styles from hop forward pales and IPAs, to malt driven brown ales and porters; from yeast led Saisons, to beers inspired by place, era, or a specific ingredient All of our beers are unfiltered and unpasteurised, with the majority naturally conditioned by yeast in keg and bottle
